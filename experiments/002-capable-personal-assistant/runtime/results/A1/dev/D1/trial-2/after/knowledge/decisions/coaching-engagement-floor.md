# Coaching Engagement Floor

Policy, adopted February 2026.

Vega Coaching does not take one-off or short coaching engagements priced under
2,500 USD. Short engagements consumed as much setup as a retainer but returned
far less.

## Rule

- Any coaching engagement (intensive, package, or one-off) priced below 2,500
  USD is declined.
- Instead, refer the prospect to the monthly retainer (3,200 USD per month) or
  to a minimum 6-week intensive at 3,000 USD.
- Existing signed retainers above the floor (e.g. Halcyon at 3,200 USD/month)
  are unaffected.
