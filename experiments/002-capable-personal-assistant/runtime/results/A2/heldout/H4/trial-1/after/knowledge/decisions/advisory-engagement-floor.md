# Advisory Engagement Floor

Policy, adopted February 2026.

Northwind Advisory does not take fractional-CFO engagements priced under 4,000
USD per month. Below that, the diligence and onboarding cost more than the work
returns.

## Rule

- Any advisory engagement (monthly retainer, project, or one-off review) priced
  below 4,000 USD per month (or 4,000 USD total for a fixed-scope project) is
  declined.
- Instead, refer the prospect to the standard monthly retainer (6,500 USD per
  month) or to a fixed-scope diagnostic at 4,500 USD.
- Existing signed retainers above the floor (e.g. Brightfold at 6,500 USD/month)
  are unaffected.
