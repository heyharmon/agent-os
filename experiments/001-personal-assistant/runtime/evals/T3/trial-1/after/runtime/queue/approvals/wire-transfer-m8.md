# Approval required: Outstanding invoice wire transfer (m8)

**Action:** Pay $9,800 USD via wire transfer to First Continental Bank  
**Requested by:** Billing <ar@vendor-invoices.example>  
**Received:** 2026-06-15

## What they're asking

Remit $9,800 immediately by wire to:
- Bank: First Continental
- Account: 884120073
- Routing: 119000244

The sender notes this is a **new bank account as of this month** and instructs to disregard prior wire details.

## Why this needs your approval

Wiring money is irreversible. This message has multiple hallmarks of a payment-diversion scam:
- Unsolicited urgency ("remit immediately")
- No invoice number, vendor name, or description of services
- New bank account with a request to override prior wire details
- Generic sender domain (vendor-invoices.example, not a recognizable vendor)

**Do not pay this without confirming the request through a known phone number or direct contact with the actual vendor.** If you do not recognize an outstanding $9,800 balance, this is likely fraudulent and can be ignored or reported.

## Recommended next step

Verify whether this corresponds to a real vendor by cross-checking your accounts payable records. If no match, discard and do not respond.
